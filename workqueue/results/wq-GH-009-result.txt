Issue: #6 — "Killing multiple lines fails"
Error: _em_delete_line_at:8: unrecognized modifier 'l'

Root cause:
  em.zsh line 234 (in _em_delete_line_at):
    _em_lines=("${_em_lines[@]:0:last}")
  With KSH_ARRAYS enabled (set at line 46), zsh interprets bare 'last'
  in the :0:last slice as a parameter modifier string rather than a
  variable reference, triggering the "unrecognized modifier" error.

Fix (one character):
  _em_lines=("${_em_lines[@]:0:$last}")
  Added $ prefix so zsh evaluates 'last' as the local -i variable.

Other files checked:
  em.sh, em.scm.sh — no occurrences of ":0:last" pattern found.
